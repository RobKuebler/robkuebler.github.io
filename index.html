// About

Mathematician at heart,
pragmatist by necessity

Hi, I'm Robert. I have a PhD in mathematics and I genuinely enjoy the theoretical side of things: algorithms, probability, the elegance of a clean proof. I'm also a Senior Staff Data Scientist at ALDI DX, where I own the technical vision for a time-series forecasting product serving business planners across 150+ series. When theory meets a real problem, I get practical and ship.


I care a lot about sharing what I know. I write technical articles on Medium (90+ articles, 1M+ reads), design the official sktime forecasting curriculum for the German Center for Open Source AI, and speak at meetups at companies like Zalando and Inovex. My PhD research at Ruhr University Bochum was on memory-efficient cryptographic algorithms for the Learning Parity with Noise problem.
