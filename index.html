// About

Where theory meets production

Hi, I'm Robert. I have a PhD in mathematics and I genuinely enjoy the theoretical side of things: algorithms, probability, the elegance of a clean proof. My PhD research at Ruhr University Bochum was on memory-efficient cryptographic algorithms for the Learning Parity with Noise problem. I'm also a Senior Staff Data Scientist at ALDI DX, where I own the technical vision for a time-series forecasting product serving business planners across 150+ series. When theory meets a real problem, I get practical and ship.


I care a lot about sharing what I know. I write technical articles on Medium (90+ articles, 1M+ reads), design the official sktime forecasting curriculum for the German Center for Open Source AI, speak at meetups at companies like Zalando and Inovex, and contribute to open-source libraries like scikit-lego and PyMC-Marketing. I occasionally take on selected freelance projects; feel free to reach out.
